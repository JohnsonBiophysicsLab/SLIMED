\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Bookkeeps simulation data throughout iterations. 

This class stores vectors of surface area, energy, and mean force values for each iteration of a simulation.

Prints information about a single iteration of the optimization algorithm. 

This function prints information about a single iteration of the optimization algorithm, including the current step number ({\ttfamily iteration}), the total energy of the mesh at that step ({\ttfamily energy\+Vec\mbox{[}iteration\mbox{]}.energy\+Total}), the mean force on the mesh vertices at that step ({\ttfamily mean\+Force\mbox{[}iteration\mbox{]}}), and the surface area of the mesh at that step ({\ttfamily area\+Total\mbox{[}iteration\mbox{]}}).

The output is sent to standard output (i.\+e., to the terminal).
